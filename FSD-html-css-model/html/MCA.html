Course Details

PS College of Technology MCA Program is accredited by NBA. The 2-year MCA program is ideal for graduates who want to make their career in the IT Sector. The admission to MCA Course is done through the TANCET Exam. After the Entrance Test result is out, a separate merit list is prepared considering the TANCET marks and then online counselling is held. The TANCET 2021 scorecard was released by Anna University on April 8, 2021, and counselling is expected to start soon. The expected cut-off for admission to MCA Program is 51 marks in TANCET 2021.Check  PSG College of Technology Admission 2021

Mini Project/Internship: Every student will have to submit a report on the Mini Project/Internship on time. The student will also have to present seminars about the progress of the Mini Project/Internship. The seminars will be presented before a review committee constituted by the HOD. The Mini Project/Internship will be evaluated based on the performance, seminars, report and a viva-voce examination.

Laboratory Courses:Every laboratory course will be evaluated based on the conduct of experiments/exercises/mini projects/development of software packages and reports.
