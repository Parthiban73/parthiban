PSCT Quick Updates

PS College of Technology (PSCT) is an autonomous private engineering college in Coimbatore, India. It is affiliated with Anna University. PSGCT admission 2022 is ongoing for the BTech program. TNEA 2022 Supplementary registrations have been reopened. Eligible candidates can register online till November 16, 2022. TNEA 2022 Supplementary rank list wil be released on November 18 and counselling will close on November 25. Further, TANCET 2023 exam dates have been announced. TANCET 2023 exam for MCA/ M.Tech/ M.Plan/ M.Arch will be held on February 25 and TANCET 2023 exam for MBA will be conducted on February 26, 2023.

PS College of Technology commonly known as PSGCT was established in 1951 as the first private Engineering Institution in Tamil Nadu by G.R. Damodaran. It is affiliated to Anna University and is located in Coimbatore. PSGCT has been ranked 63rd by NIRF 2022 in the Management Category. Popularly PSGCT offers courses in Science & Technology, Engineering, and Management. B.E. & B.Tech admissions are based on TNEA Counselling. While M.E, M.Tech, MBA, PGDM, and MCA are based on TANCET followed by TANCA Counselling. GATE is also accepted for M.E. and M.Tech. The PhD admissions at the college are based on GATE / NET / JRF / PSGCT Entrance scores.
